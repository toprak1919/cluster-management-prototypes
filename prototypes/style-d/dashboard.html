THE AFRICA MULTIPLE CLUSTER OF EXCELLENCE — UNIVERSITY OF BAYREUTH

LEAD STORY

847 persons across 38 countries form the backbone of the Africa Multiple Cluster of Excellence. This unprecedented network of scholars spans disciplines from migration studies to postcolonial theory, united by a shared commitment to rethinking African multiplicities and their global interconnections.

The cluster continues to grow with 12 new members joining this quarter alone, bringing fresh perspectives from institutions across West Africa, East Africa, and the diaspora. Three new research projects have been submitted for funding approval this month.
